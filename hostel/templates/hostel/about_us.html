HOSTEL MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

The 'Hostel Management System' thus is an idea born from the same thought process. It is a modern way of maintaining hostel-realted data, something that is completely different from the traditional pen and paper method. It is easy to implement and it reduces manual management work. It is more secured as compared to traditional data entry. Updation, Deletion and linking of student data is efficient and effective. It automates repeated tasks like student registration,visitor details,etc.

This website provides the following functionalities:
1. Student Registration
2. Visitor Details
3. Updation of Student Information
4. Room Details
